Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Ball Forge ("App" or "Service"). By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only information necessary to provide, improve, and secure the Service.

  • Account Information: If you choose to sign in using Apple ID, Google, Facebook, or other supported login methods, we may receive your email address, display name, and profile picture for account creation, progress synchronization, and support purposes.
  • Device Information: We may collect device model, operating system version, language settings, IP address, time zone, application version, screen resolution, and advertising identifiers (IDFA) to improve compatibility and prevent abuse.
  • Gameplay Information: We may collect gameplay progress, completed levels, achievements, session duration, in-game actions, and feature usage statistics to improve gameplay experience and balance.
  • Analytics and Diagnostic Data: Crash reports, performance logs, and diagnostic information may be collected automatically to help identify bugs and improve app stability.
  • Advertising Information: Advertising partners may use advertising identifiers to provide personalized advertisements and measure advertising effectiveness.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain gameplay services.
  • Save and synchronize user progress.
  • Improve gameplay quality and app performance.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and technical issues.
  • Deliver relevant advertisements.
  • Analyze user behavior to improve future content.
  • Notify users of important updates and policy changes.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers supporting analytics, advertising, cloud storage, and app functionality.
  • When required by applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.
  • To protect the security, rights, and safety of users and the Service.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or business transfer.
  • In anonymized or aggregated formats that do not identify individuals.

4. Data Security

  • Encrypted data transmission using secure protocols.
  • Restricted access to sensitive information.
  • Regular security monitoring and maintenance.
  • Industry-standard safeguards against unauthorized access.

While we take reasonable measures to protect information, no security method can guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Account information is retained while your account remains active.
  • Gameplay data is retained to maintain progress and achievements.
  • Analytics and diagnostic data may be retained for service improvement purposes.
  • Information required by law may be retained for longer periods where necessary.
